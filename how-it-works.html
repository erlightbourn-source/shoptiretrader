How It Works

Simple for buyers, simple for sellers

No auctions, no shipping headaches, no dealer markup — just local buyers and sellers connecting direct.

Browse listingsSell your tires

Buying a set

Find tires near you and deal straight with the person selling them.

Search your size and city

Enter the size off your sidewall (something like 235/45R18) and your area. You'll see what local sellers have available right now.

Message the seller

Ask what matters: tread left, how old they are, why they're selling. It's a normal conversation, not a bidding war.

Meet up and look them over

Pick a public spot close to you, check the tires in person, and only pay once you're happy with them.

Selling a set

Turn tires you're not using into cash from a local buyer.

Snap a few photos and list

A handful of clear pictures, the size, and honest condition. It takes a couple of minutes.

Name your price

You decide what they're worth. No dealer setting the number for you.

Talk to local buyers

Interested drivers reach out directly. Answer their questions and agree on a time.

Meet and get paid

Hand off the tires and take the cash. Founding Sellers ride at the top of local results while doing it.
