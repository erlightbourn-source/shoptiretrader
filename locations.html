Why buy tires local in South Florida

Skip the shipping and the dealer markup.

Buying tires from someone in your own city means no freight charges, no waiting on a delivery, and no mounting fees stacked on top of a padded price. You see the tires before you pay, and if a size doesn't work out, the next set is usually a short drive away.

Tire Trader covers Broward end to end, from Deerfield Beach and Coconut Creek up north, through Coral Springs, Pompano, Sunrise, Plantation and Davie in the middle, down to Hollywood and Pembroke Pines in the south. Coverage in Miami-Dade and Palm Beach is growing as more local sellers come on. Whether you're after a full new set, a pair to match, or clean takeoffs off a recent trade-in, you'll be dealing with a neighbor, not a warehouse three states away.
