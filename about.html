Why we built Tire Trader

A tire marketplace for people who actually live and drive in South Florida.

Tire Trader started with a familiar annoyance: needing a couple of tires in a hurry and getting quoted dealer money for rubber that was sitting brand new in a warehouse a few towns over.

Meanwhile, shops around Broward have good takeoff sets piling up every week, and plenty of drivers try to sell barely-used tires online only to get lowballed by out-of-town flippers. There was an obvious gap in the middle, so we built for it.

Tire Trader is a marketplace just for tires, run for the people who buy and sell them here. List a set, find a set, and meet up in your own city. No national listing dump, no shipping a 40-pound tire across the country, and nobody taking a cut in between.

We're Broward-first and growing across South Florida as more sellers and buyers show up. If you've got tires to move or a car that needs them, that's exactly who we made this for.
